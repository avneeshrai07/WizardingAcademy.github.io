CHARMS

Charms comprised of a very wide range of different spells concerned with giving a target (be it an object or an individual) new and unexpected properties and/or making the target perform certain actions, among other possible effects.

Defence Against the Dark Arts

Defence Against the Dark Arts was class that taught students how to protect themselves against the Dark Arts and how to use offencive and protective spells, and how to properly handle and deal with dark creatures.

Muggle Studies

Muggle Studies was a class that taught about how Muggles lived their lives, as well as various Muggle inventions and their use of electricity. It also included Muggle Art and Muggle Music. However, the information came from a wizarding point of view.

Labs

Hogwarts Labs, nestled within the mystical and enchanting walls of Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, is a place of magical experimentation and potion-making mastery. This hallowed space is dedicated to the art and science of concocting potions, an essential discipline in the education of aspiring witches and wizards.

Campus

The Hogwarts campus is renowned for its majestic and enchanting architecture, featuring towering spires, grand towers, and mysterious hidden passages. The castle is surrounded by vast grounds, including the Forbidden Forest and the Black Lake. The Quidditch pitch, where the magical sport of Quidditch is played, adds to the vibrant and lively atmosphere.

Hostel

The Hogwarts hostels, also known as houses, are Gryffindor, Hufflepuff, Ravenclaw, and Slytherin. Each hostel has its unique characteristics and values, and students are sorted into one of these houses upon their arrival at Hogwarts.
